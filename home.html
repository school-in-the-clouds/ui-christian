Welcome To School In The Cloud

At School In The Cloud, we pride ourselves on getting the best teacher to student ratios possible. We are planning on expanding to 20 new cities in the upcoming year so keep an eye out because we could be in your city! We help students learn with after school tutoring every day (including weekends) and will always shoot for the moon!

Overview

Our goal is to provide the highest possible student to teacher ratio to help studetns truly become all they can be and excell in their education. We offer tutoring on a variety of subjects from K-12 and are adding more every day!

Volunteers

Our volunteers have a background in education and enjoy teaching and helping young minds to learn over a variety of subjects. If you're interested in becoming a volunteer at Cloud In The Sky, click the "lean more" button found below.

Student

We believe in only the best for out students; that's why then you join Cloud In The Sky, you'll be getting the best help we can provide. All students are assigned a specific volunteer to answer any questions as well as another volunteer for their study groups. Study groups are always as small as possible, 1 on 1 if possible.

Admin

Our wonderful admins will reach out to each student and find out what subjects you need the most help in and how you best learn; be it visually, auditory, or hands on, and match you with the best volunteer for the job! Admins also get to know the Volunteers and set them up in their own roles.

Learn With Us!

To get started with Cloud In The Sky, all you have to do is click the button below that says "sign up" and you're off! There are no fees to join Cloud In The Sky and there never will be! We are a free service to help you or your child succeed in school!
